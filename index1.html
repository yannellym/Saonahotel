State of the art rooms

Rooms where one gets to marvel at modern minimalist decor in its full glory—plus, the hotel's facade is well-maintained and painted in the same colorscheme to match the interiors. While most might find green and blue a little repetitive, it is a welcome sight from the colorful cityscape of Puerto Plata. Our rooms are equipped with 65" flat screen TVs, surround sound sytem, Alexa, and much more.

Local and native food

Visiting Saona Hotel entails discovering all of the Dominican Republic's flavors: Tropical fruits of the Taino people; the beef, pork, oregano and saffron brought on Spanish ships in the 15th century; and the techniques and ingredients (such as bananas and yams) of African slaves, resulting in local dishes such as mofongo and mangú. Traditional Dominican food is a fusion that makes anyone’s mouth water.
